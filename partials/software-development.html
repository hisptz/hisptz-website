Software development

Software development and integration is a continuous activity that is handled by the UDSM/UiO-MOHSW team. The UDSM/UiO-MOHSW team is responsible for
development and customization of the district based National Data Warehouse on the DHIS2 platform. The team is involved with

Designing databases and software for 'rapid rollout' based on existing, but harmonized, data collection tools.
Updating software and data warehouse designs based on revised tools.
Integrating DHIS2 with other related applications (e.g. LGMD).
Customizing easy to access and use web portals and HTML reports to encourage information use.
