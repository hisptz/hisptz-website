Predictive analytics in innovating road safety

1 year, 4 months ago mukulu

It is a business intelligence technology used by most fortune 500 companies across the globe through a number of techniques from data mining and patterns modeling to analyze current and future historical facts to make predictions about the future and events that are yet to unfold.

Most companies and institutions today, spend a fortune on Information systems to help running day to day operations, management and planning. This has enabled availability of enough backlog of data opening avenues for a lot of untapped potential in gaining future insights on market directions and production trends and lynch pin into sustainable institutional growth.

In road safety Predictive analytics gives the ability to utilize data to create holistic view of road offenses, accidents, pinpoint root causes and predict potential major concerns before they become problematic. Complexity of road safety environment requires a solution that the ability to analyze all types of data from all sources within and outside the road safety industry, Ranging from census on population data, to health domain and law enforcement.

Deduction of association between increased accidents trends and major health issues pertained to a certain age groups of drivers and articulation of major traffic offenses to increased influx of motor vehicles can be well understood through crunching of enough backlog data from multiple information sources.

With iRoad 2.0, we wanted to design and build an incredibly simple applications, but without compromise. Everything we've researched in developing really friendly interfaces and everything we've researched in developing powerful data mining and warehouse tools, culminates in iRoad 2.0 infrastructure. It is the most powerful and scalable software we've ever engineered.
